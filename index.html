Rename a file before sharing it — perfect for uploading to Nextcloud with a meaningful name.

How to use

1
Install this app.
In Chrome, tap the menu (⋮) and choose Add to Home Screen. This registers the app in your Android share sheet.
2
Share a file to RenameAndShare.
Open your Downloads or Files app, long-press a file, tap Share, and pick RenameAndShare from the share sheet.
3
Choose a new name.
Use the Composed mode to build a name like 260312-My-Purchase-receipt.pdf, or switch to Freetext for full control.
4
Tap Rename & Share.
The share sheet opens again with the renamed file. Pick the Nextcloud app — it will let you choose the target folder and upload.
No files are ever uploaded or stored by this app. Renaming and sharing happens entirely on your device.
